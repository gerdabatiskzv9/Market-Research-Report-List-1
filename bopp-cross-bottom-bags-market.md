<p><p>BOPP cross bottom bags are versatile packaging solutions that come in two main types: valve cross bottom bags and open cross bottom bags. Valve cross bottom bags feature a built-in valve that allows for efficient filling and minimizes product waste, making them ideal for powdered materials. In contrast, open cross bottom bags have an open top, allowing easy access for filling and are commonly used for bulk items. Both types offer strength and durability, catering to various industrial and retail applications.</p></p>
