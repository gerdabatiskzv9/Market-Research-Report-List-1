<p><p>Multi Service Chilled Beams (MSCBs) are advanced HVAC systems that provide efficient cooling and heating in commercial and residential buildings. Additionally, advancements in technology, such as smart controls and improved design, are contributing to market expansion.</p><p>Current trends indicate a preference for integrating MSCBs with building management systems, promoting centralized control and enhancing user experience.
