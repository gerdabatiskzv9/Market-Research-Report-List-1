<p><ul><li>Standard Specification</li><li>Large Wave Pattern</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corrugated steel sheets are primarily categorized into two market types: standard specification and large wave pattern. The standard specification sheets feature uniform, closely spaced waves, providing strength and durability suitable for roofing and siding applications. In contrast, large wave pattern sheets exhibit broader, deeper corrugations, offering enhanced aesthetics and unique visual appeal, often used in architectural designs and artistic projects. Both types serve diverse construction needs, balancing structural integrity with design preferences across various industries.</p></p>
