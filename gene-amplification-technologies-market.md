<p><p>Gene amplification technologies are essential for detecting and analyzing genetic material. The Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) allows for the exponential amplification of DNA sequences, enabling precise genetic analysis. Loop Mediated Isothermal Amplification (LAMP) works under isothermal conditions, making it simpler and faster than PCR, suitable for point-of-care applications. Other market technologies include Rolling Circle Amplification (RCA) and Nested PCR, which cater to specific research needs. These methods collectively enhance genetic research and diagnostic capabilities across various fields.</p></p>
