<p><ul><li>Single Track Manual Tile Cutter</li><li>Double Track Manual Tile Cutter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The manual tile cutter market consists of two primary types: single-track and double-track manual tile cutters. Single-track manual tile cutters feature a single rail for guiding the cutting wheel, making them suitable for smaller projects and lighter tiles. In contrast, double-track manual tile cutters possess dual rails, offering enhanced stability and precision, ideal for larger projects and thicker tiles. Both types are essential for DIY enthusiasts and professionals, providing efficient cutting options tailored to varying tile sizes and applications.</p></p>
