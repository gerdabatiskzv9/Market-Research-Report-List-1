<p><ul><li>Manual Pressure Infusion Set</li><li>Automatic Pressure Infusion Set</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pressure Infusion Set market is divided into two primary types: manual and automatic pressure infusion sets. Manual pressure infusion sets require healthcare personnel to operate a squeeze bag to maintain constant pressure for fluid delivery, offering control and flexibility during procedures. In contrast, automatic pressure infusion sets utilize mechanical or electronic systems to regulate pressure automatically, enhancing efficiency and reducing the risk of human error. Both types serve critical roles in emergency medicine and fluid management in various clinical settings.</p></p>
