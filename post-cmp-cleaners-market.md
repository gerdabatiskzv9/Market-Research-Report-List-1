<p><p>The Post CMP Cleaners market is categorized into two main types: acidic and alkaline cleaners. Acidic post CMP cleaners are designed to remove metal contaminants and oxide layers, effectively stabilizing pH levels on semiconductor wafers. They promote enhanced surface cleanliness and prevent defects. Alkaline post CMP cleaners, on the other hand, target organic residues and particulate matter, ensuring a thorough cleaning process. Both types are essential for maintaining the integrity and performance of semiconductor devices in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
