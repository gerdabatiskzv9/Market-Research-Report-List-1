<p><p>Capacitive keyboards are innovative input devices that utilize capacitive sensing technology to detect touch. Unlike traditional mechanical keyboards, which rely on physical switches, capacitive keyboards register keystrokes based on changes in electric fields when a finger approaches or touches the key surface.
