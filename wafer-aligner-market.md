<p><ul><li>Vacuum Aligner</li><li>Atmosphere Aligner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wafer aligner market comprises two primary types: vacuum aligners and atmosphere aligners. Vacuum aligners use a controlled vacuum environment to precisely position wafers, ensuring high alignment accuracy critical for advanced semiconductor fabrication. In contrast, atmosphere aligners operate under ambient conditions, offering simpler setups and greater flexibility for various applications. Both types serve essential roles in the semiconductor manufacturing process, facilitating the accurate layering of materials needed for integrated circuits and other electronic components.</p></p>
