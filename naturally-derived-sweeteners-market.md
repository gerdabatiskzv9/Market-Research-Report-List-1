<p><p>The naturally derived sweeteners market is segmented into three primary types: powder, liquid, and crystals. Powdered sweeteners offer convenience and shelf stability, making them popular for baking and cooking. Liquid sweeteners, such as agave nectar and honey, provide ease of use in beverages and sauces, enhancing flavor without needing to dissolve. Crystal sweeteners, derived from natural sources like sugar cane or beet, mimic traditional granulated sugar and are versatile for various culinary applications, appealing to health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
