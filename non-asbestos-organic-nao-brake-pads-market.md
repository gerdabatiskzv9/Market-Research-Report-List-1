<p><p>The Non-Asbestos Organic (NAO) brake pads market is primarily divided into two types: copper-free and copper-bearing. Copper-free brake pads utilize organic materials like rubber and cellulose, promoting environmental safety by eliminating copper, which can pollute waterways. On the other hand, copper-bearing brake pads incorporate copper fibers for enhanced braking performance and heat dissipation. However, concerns about copper's environmental impact have led to a shift towards copper-free options, influencing industry trends and regulatory compliance in the automotive sector.</p></p>
