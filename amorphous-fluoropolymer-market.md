<p><p>The amorphous fluoropolymer market can be segmented into solid and solution types. Solid amorphous fluoropolymers are used in applications requiring high thermal stability and chemical resistance, often utilized in coatings and industrial components. In contrast, solution amorphous fluoropolymers are provided in liquid form, making them suitable for applications that require uniform application and better adhesion, such as paints and sealants.
