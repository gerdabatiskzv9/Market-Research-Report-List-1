<p><ul><li>Through Hole Technology</li><li>Surface Mount Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bypass diode market is segmented into two main types: Through Hole Technology (THT) and Surface Mount Technology (SMT). THT involves diodes that are inserted into pre-drilled holes on a circuit board, offering durability and ease of repair. SMT, on the other hand, features diodes mounted directly onto the surface of the board, allowing for compact designs and automation in manufacturing. Both technologies cater to different applications, influencing factors like cost, size, and assembly processes in the market.</p></p>
