<p><p>The metal milling machine market can be classified into two main types: manual control and automatic control. Manual control machines require operators to guide and adjust the milling process, providing greater hands-on involvement and flexibility for intricate tasks. In contrast, automatic control machines are equipped with computer numerical control (CNC) systems, allowing for precise, repeatable operations with minimal human intervention. This enhances efficiency, reduces labor costs, and improves accuracy, catering to varying production needs in the industry.</p></p>
