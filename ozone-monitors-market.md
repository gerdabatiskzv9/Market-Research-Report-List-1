<p><strong>The Ozone Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>UV Photometric</li><li>Electrochemical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ozone monitors market primarily features two types: UV photometric and electrochemical monitors. UV photometric monitors utilize ultraviolet light to measure ozone concentration by detecting the absorption of UV rays, providing high sensitivity and accuracy. In contrast, electrochemical monitors function by measuring the electrical current produced during the interaction of ozone with a sensing electrode, offering a cost-effective and portable solution. Both types play a critical role in environmental monitoring and pollution control, tailored for various applications and budgets.</p></p>
