<p><p>The biometric cards market is divided into two main types: contact biometric cards and contactless biometric cards. Contact biometric cards require physical contact with a reader, incorporating fingerprint scanning technology to enhance security during transactions. In contrast, contactless biometric cards utilize Near Field Communication (NFC) or similar technologies, allowing users to authenticate their identity by simply waving the card near a reader. Both types aim to provide secure and convenient methods for identity verification across various applications, including banking and access control.</p></p>
