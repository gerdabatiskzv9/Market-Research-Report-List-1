<p><strong>The Medical Liquid Chromatography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Liquid Chromatography</li><li>Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Liquid Chromatography market comprises two main types: Liquid Chromatography (LC) and Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry (LC-MS). LC is used for separating and analyzing compounds in biological samples, making it essential in diagnostics and research. LC-MS combines chromatographic separation with mass spectrometry, enhancing sensitivity and specificity, allowing for detailed analysis of complex mixtures, such as proteins and metabolites. Both methods are crucial for drug development, clinical diagnostics, and pharmaceutical quality control, driving advancements in personalized medicine.</p></p>
