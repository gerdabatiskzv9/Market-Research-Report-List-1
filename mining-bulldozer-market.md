<p><p>The mining bulldozer market features two main types: wheeled bulldozers and crawler bulldozers. Wheeled bulldozers are characterized by their rubber tires, providing higher speed and mobility on firm surfaces, making them suitable for quick, less demanding tasks. In contrast, crawler bulldozers utilize tracked systems, offering superior stability and traction on uneven or soft terrain, making them ideal for heavy-duty operations in rugged mining environments. Both types serve vital roles in material handling and site preparation within the mining industry.</p></p>
