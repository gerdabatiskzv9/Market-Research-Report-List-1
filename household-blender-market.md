<p><p>The household blender market encompasses three primary types: countertop blenders, portable blenders, and hand blenders. Countertop blenders are versatile, ideal for blending larger quantities and tougher ingredients, making them suitable for smoothies and soups. Portable blenders offer convenience and mobility, allowing users to blend on-the-go, often powered by batteries. Hand blenders, also known as immersion blenders, are compact and designed for directly blending in pots or bowls, providing ease of use and quick cleanup for small tasks.</p></p>
