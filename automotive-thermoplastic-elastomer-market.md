SBS and SEBS are favored for their flexibility and resilience, making them ideal for interior applications. TPO offers excellent thermal stability and chemical resistance, often used in exterior parts. TPU combines elasticity with strength, suitable for parts requiring durability. Additionally, other thermoplastic elastomers provide specialized properties that cater to diverse automotive applications, enhancing overall performance and aesthetics.</p></p>
