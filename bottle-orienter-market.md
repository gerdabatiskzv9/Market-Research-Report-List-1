<p><ul><li>Inline Bottle Orienter</li><li>Rotary Container Orienter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Bottle Orienter market is segmented into two main types: Inline Bottle Orienters and Rotary Container Orienters. Inline Bottle Orienters streamline the orientation of bottles on a linear production line, enhancing efficiency for high-speed bottling operations. In contrast, Rotary Container Orienters utilize a circular mechanism to orient bottles, ideal for moderate production speeds where space is a consideration. Both types are essential for ensuring proper alignment before filling and labeling, optimizing overall packaging processes in various industries.</p></p>
