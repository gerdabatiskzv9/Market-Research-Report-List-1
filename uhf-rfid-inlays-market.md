<p><p>The UHF RFID inlays market is divided primarily into two types: UHF dry inlays and UHF wet inlays. UHF dry inlays consist of RFID chips and antennae adhered to a substrate without any adhesive layer, making them suitable for applications requiring a flat profile, such as labels. In contrast, UHF wet inlays integrate adhesive and are used for tagging items in various environments, offering more flexibility for application on curved or irregular surfaces. Both types serve diverse sectors, enhancing inventory management and tracking processes.</p></p>
