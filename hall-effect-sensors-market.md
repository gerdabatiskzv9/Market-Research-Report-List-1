<p><strong>The Hall-Effect Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Threshold Type</li><li>Linear Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hall-effect sensors are categorized into two main market types: threshold type and linear type. Threshold type sensors detect magnetic fields and provide a binary output, activating at a specific threshold, ideal for applications like proximity sensing and switching. In contrast, linear type sensors produce an output that varies linearly with the strength of the magnetic field, making them suitable for precise measurements of position and speed in applications such as motor control and tilt sensing.</p></p>
