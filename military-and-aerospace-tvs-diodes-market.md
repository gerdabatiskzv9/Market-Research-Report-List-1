<p><p>The Military and Aerospace TVS Diodes market features two primary types: uni-polar and bi-polar TVS diodes. Uni-polar TVS diodes are designed for unidirectional transient suppression, effectively protecting sensitive components against voltage spikes in one direction. In contrast, bi-polar TVS diodes cater to bidirectional applications, safeguarding circuits that may experience voltage transients in both polarities. Both types are essential in military and aerospace applications, where reliability and performance under extreme conditions are critical for protecting electronic systems from surges and transients.</p></p>
