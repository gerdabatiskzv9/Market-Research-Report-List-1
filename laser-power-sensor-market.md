<p><p>The laser power sensor market comprises various types, including thermopile sensors and photodiode sensors. Thermopile sensors measure laser power by detecting temperature changes caused by absorbed laser energy, offering high sensitivity and broad wavelength ranges. Photodiode sensors operate by converting light into electrical current, providing fast response times and accuracy for lower power levels. Other types may include semiconductor sensors and pyroelectric sensors, each designed for specific applications and characteristics, catering to diverse industry requirements in laser power measurement.</p></p>
