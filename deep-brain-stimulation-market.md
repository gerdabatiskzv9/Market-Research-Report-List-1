<p><p>The Deep Brain Stimulation (DBS) market comprises three main components: the implanted pulse generator, the lead, and the extension. The implanted pulse generator is the device that delivers electrical impulses to specific brain areas, facilitating therapeutic effects. The lead, a thin electrode, is inserted into the targeted brain region to conduct these impulses. The extension connects the lead to the pulse generator, allowing for flexibility in positioning and optimizing the delivery of stimulation for effective treatment of neurological conditions.</p></p>
