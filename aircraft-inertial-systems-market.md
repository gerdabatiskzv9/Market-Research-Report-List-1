<p><strong>The Aircraft Inertial Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AHRS Type</li><li>INS Type</li><li>IMU Type</li><li>laser Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Inertial Systems market comprises several types: </p><p>1. **AHRS Type**: Attitude and Heading Reference Systems provide spatial orientation using sensors to measure angular velocity. </p><p>2. **INS Type**: Inertial Navigation Systems utilize accelerometers and gyroscopes for continuous position data without external references. </p><p>3. **IMU Type**: Inertial Measurement Units measure specific force and angular rate, essential for aircraft navigation and stabilization. </p><p>4. **Laser Type**: Laser-based inertial systems offer high precision positioning using laser technology. </p><p>5. **Others**: This category includes hybrid systems and emerging technologies enhancing navigation capabilities.</p></p>
