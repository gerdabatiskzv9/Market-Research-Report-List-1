<p><p>The die attach adhesive film market is categorized into two primary types: non-conductive and conductive. Non-conductive adhesive films are primarily used for applications requiring electrical insulation, providing reliable bonding without electrical interference. In contrast, conductive adhesive films contain conductive materials, allowing them to facilitate electrical connections between components. Both types play critical roles in semiconductor packaging, impacting performance, reliability, and thermal management in electronic devices. The choice between them depends on specific application requirements and environmental considerations.</p></p>
