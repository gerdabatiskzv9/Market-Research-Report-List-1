<p><ul><li>Analog Type</li><li>Digital Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft tilt sensors are classified into two main types: analog and digital. Analog tilt sensors provide continuous voltage outputs proportional to the tilt angle, offering smooth and real-time readings. They are simpler and often more cost-effective but may lack precision in certain applications. Digital tilt sensors, on the other hand, convert tilt measurements into digital signals, enabling higher accuracy and integration with modern electronic systems. Digital sensors often feature advanced features like data logging and communication capabilities, appealing to more sophisticated aircraft systems.</p></p>
