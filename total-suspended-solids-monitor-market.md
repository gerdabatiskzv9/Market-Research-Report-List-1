<p><p>The Total Suspended Solids (TSS) monitor market is primarily divided into two types: submersible probes and insert probes. Submersible probes are designed to operate underwater, offering real-time measurements of suspended solids in various aquatic environments, making them ideal for wastewater management and environmental monitoring. On the other hand, insert probes are installed within pipelines or treatment systems, allowing for continuous monitoring without direct exposure to the water, providing accurate data for industrial processes and efficiency optimization.</p></p>
