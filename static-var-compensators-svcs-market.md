<p><p>Static Var Compensators (SVCs) are flexible AC transmission systems that enhance power system stability by regulating voltage levels and reactive power. The market for SVCs is segmented into Thyristor-Based and Magnetically Controlled Reactor (MCR)-Based systems. Thyristor-Based SVCs utilize thyristors for rapid response and precise control of reactive power, while MCR-Based systems employ a magnetically controlled reactor to adjust reactive power more gradually. Both technologies contribute to efficient grid management, improving reliability and performance in electrical systems.</p></p>
