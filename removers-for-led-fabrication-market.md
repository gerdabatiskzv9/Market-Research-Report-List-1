<p><strong>The Removers for LED Fabrication Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoresist Residue Remover</li><li>Plasma Residue Remover</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED fabrication market utilizes various removers to eliminate residues during the production process. Photoresist residue removers are specifically designed to clean surfaces of leftover photoresist materials used in photolithography, ensuring optimal performance of the LEDs. Plasma residue removers, on the other hand, target and eliminate byproducts generated during plasma etching processes, which are crucial for precise patterning. Both types play a vital role in maintaining the quality and efficiency of LED manufacturing by ensuring clean substrates for subsequent layers.</p></p>
