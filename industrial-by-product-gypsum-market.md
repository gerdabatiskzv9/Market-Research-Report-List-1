<p><p>The industrial by-product gypsum market includes several types, each derived from different processes. Phosphogypsum originates from phosphate fertilizer production, while fluorogypsum is a by-product of the fluorine industry. Flue gas desulfurization gypsum is generated from power plants that remove sulfur dioxide, enhancing environmental benefits. Gypsum citrate, created from citric acid production, offers agricultural applications, while salt gypsum arises from salt production processes. Each type serves various industries, including construction, agriculture, and environmental management, contributing to sustainable practices.</p></p>
