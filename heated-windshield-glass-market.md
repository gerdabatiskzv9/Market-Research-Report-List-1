<p><strong>The Heated Windshield Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heated Wire Windshield</li><li>Heated Coated Windshield</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heated windshield glass market is primarily categorized into two types: heated wire windshields and heated coated windshields. Heated wire windshields use embedded conductive wires to generate heat, preventing frost and ice accumulation. Conversely, heated coated windshields employ a transparent conductive film or coating that evenly distributes heat across the glass surface. Both types enhance driver visibility and safety in cold weather, making them popular in the automotive industry, particularly in regions with harsh winter conditions.</p></p>
