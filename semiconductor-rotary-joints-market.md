<p><p>The semiconductor rotary joints market encompasses two main types: single channel and multi-channel. Single channel rotary joints facilitate the transmission of fluid or electrical signals through a single pathway, making them ideal for simpler applications where data transfer requirements are minimal. In contrast, multi-channel rotary joints support multiple pathways, allowing simultaneous transmission of various signals or fluids, which is advantageous in complex systems needing efficient communication, such as robotics or advanced manufacturing processes. Both types cater to specific application needs.</p></p>
