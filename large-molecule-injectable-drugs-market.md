<p><p>The large molecule injectable drugs market encompasses various administration routes, including intravenous (IV), intramuscular (IM), and subcutaneous (SC) injections. IV injections deliver drugs directly into the bloodstream for rapid effects, ideal for emergencies or hospitals. IM injections penetrate deeper into muscle tissues, offering sustained release and effectiveness for vaccines and long-acting medications. SC injections involve administering drugs into the fatty tissue beneath the skin, allowing for slower absorption, suitable for at-home treatments and biologics like insulin and monoclonal antibodies.</p></p>
