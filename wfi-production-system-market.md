<p><p>The WFI (Water for Injection) production system includes various market types, primarily vapor compression distillation and reverse osmosis combined with ultrafiltration (UF). Vapor compression distillation is a thermal method that utilizes heat to evaporate water, condensing it into purified WFI. In contrast, the reverse osmosis and UF combination employs membrane technology to effectively remove contaminants and particulates. Both methods ensure the high purity required for pharmaceutical applications, with each offering distinct advantages in efficiency and production scalability.</p></p>
