<p><p>Workover fluid is a specialized fluid used in oil and gas well operations to maintain or restore well productivity. It is primarily utilized during workover operations, which involve interventions to repair, clean, or enhance the performance of an existing well. Workover fluids are designed to control pressure, prevent formation damage, and ensure efficient wellbore cleaning. They are typically composed of water, oil, or gas-based solutions, often mixed with additives like viscosifiers, surfactants, and corrosion inhibitors to meet specific operational requirements.</p><p>The Workover Fluid Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing demand for oil and gas, aging well infrastructure, and the need for enhanced recovery techniques. These factors are expected to drive innovation and market expansion in the coming years.</p></p>
