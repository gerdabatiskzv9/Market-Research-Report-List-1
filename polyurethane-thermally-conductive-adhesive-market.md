<p><p>The polyurethane thermally conductive adhesive market encompasses various types, notably polyurethane thermally conductive potting compounds and polyurethane thermally conductive gap fillers. Potting compounds are used to encapsulate and protect electronic components, ensuring optimal heat dissipation while providing dielectric insulation. Gap fillers, on the other hand, serve to eliminate air gaps between surfaces, enhancing thermal conductivity in assemblies. Both types play crucial roles in improving thermal management in electronic devices, automotive applications, and industrial equipment, ensuring reliable performance and longevity.</p></p>
