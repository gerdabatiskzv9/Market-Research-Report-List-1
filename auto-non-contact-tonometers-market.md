<p><p>Auto Non Contact Tonometers (ANCTs) are advanced ophthalmic devices used for measuring intraocular pressure (IOP) without the need for physical contact with the eye. With the increasing prevalence of glaucoma and other eye-related disorders, the demand for accurate and safe IOP measurement is rising.</p><p>The Auto Non Contact Tonometers Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.80% during the forecast period. Moreover, the rising geriatric population, susceptible to various ocular conditions, is driving market demand. </p><p>Additionally, growing awareness about eye health and regular checkups is contributing to the market's expansion. Emerging markets are witnessing a surge in adoption due to rising healthcare investments and improved healthcare infrastructure.
