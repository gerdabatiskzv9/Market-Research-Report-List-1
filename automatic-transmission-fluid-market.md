<p><p>The automatic transmission fluid market is categorized into semi-synthetic and full-synthetic types. Semi-synthetic automatic transmission fluid combines mineral oil with synthetic additives, offering better performance and protection than conventional fluids while being more affordable. In contrast, full-synthetic automatic transmission fluid is formulated entirely from synthetic base oils, providing superior thermal stability, enhanced lubrication, and improved fuel efficiency. Both types are designed to optimize transmission performance but cater to different vehicle requirements and consumer preferences.</p></p>
