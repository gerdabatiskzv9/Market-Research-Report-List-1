<p><strong>The Power Distribution Cables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HV</li><li>MV</li><li>LV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Power distribution cables are categorized into three main types based on voltage levels: High Voltage (HV), Medium Voltage (MV), and Low Voltage (LV). HV cables, typically above 35 kV, are used for long-distance transmission and major infrastructures. MV cables, ranging from 1 kV to 35 kV, distribute electricity to substations and industrial sectors. LV cables, below 1 kV, are primarily used for residential and commercial applications, delivering power directly to end-users safely and efficiently. Each type plays a critical role in energy infrastructure.</p></p>
