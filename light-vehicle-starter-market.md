<p><strong>The Light Vehicle Starter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light vehicle starter market is divided into two main types: direct control and electromagnetic control starters. Direct control starters utilize mechanical systems for engagement and disengagement, providing reliable performance with precise control. In contrast, electromagnetic control starters employ electromagnetic systems to activate the starter motor, allowing for smoother operation and enhanced efficiency. Both types cater to different automotive applications, with direct control offering robustness while electromagnetic control focuses on improved responsiveness and minimized energy consumption.</p></p>
