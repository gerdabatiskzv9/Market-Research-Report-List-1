<p><ul><li>Thermal-neutron Reactors</li><li>Fast-neutron Reactors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable nuclear reactor market comprises two main types: thermal-neutron reactors and fast-neutron reactors. Thermal-neutron reactors utilize slow-moving neutrons to sustain nuclear fission, making them suitable for efficient, controlled energy production. Fast-neutron reactors, on the other hand, employ high-energy neutrons, enabling them to utilize a broader range of nuclear fuels and recycle materials. Both types cater to diverse applications, including remote energy supply and military uses, capitalizing on their compact design and enhanced safety features for portable energy solutions.</p></p>
