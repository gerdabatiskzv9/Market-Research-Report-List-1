<p><p>The agricultural surfactants market is segmented into four main types: non-ionic, anionic, cationic, and amphoteric surfactants. Non-ionic surfactants are neutral and effective in a wide range of conditions. Anionic surfactants carry a negative charge, enhancing soil wetting and herbicide performance. Cationic surfactants possess a positive charge, often used for antimicrobial purposes. Amphoteric surfactants can act as either cationic or anionic depending on the pH, providing versatility in applications. Each type plays a crucial role in improving agricultural product efficacy and performance.</p></p>
