<p><p>Bench-top autoclaves are categorized based on their sterilization methods. Steam autoclaves utilize high-pressure steam to eliminate microorganisms, making them ideal for sterilizing surgical instruments. Plasma autoclaves employ low-temperature hydrogen peroxide plasma, suitable for sensitive equipment that cannot withstand high heat. Hot air autoclaves use dry heat for sterilization, effective for glassware and powders. Other types may include chemical vapor or microwave sterilizers, catering to specific needs in laboratories, dental clinics, and research facilities, ensuring diverse applications in sterilization processes.</p></p>
