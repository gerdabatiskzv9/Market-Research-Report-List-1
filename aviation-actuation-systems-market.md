<p><p>The Aviation Actuation Systems market includes three primary types: Hydraulic Drive Systems, Electric Drive Systems, and Pneumatic Drive Systems. Hydraulic systems use pressurized fluid for precise and powerful actuation, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Electric systems rely on electric motors, offering lightweight, efficient, and environmentally friendly solutions, increasingly popular in modern aircraft. Pneumatic systems utilize compressed air, providing simplicity and reliability, often used in secondary flight controls. Each system caters to specific operational needs, balancing performance, weight, and complexity in aviation applications.</p></p>
