<p><ul><li>Multi-stage Pump</li><li>Single-stage Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centrifugal impeller pumps are categorized into multi-stage and single-stage pumps based on their design and application. Single-stage pumps consist of a single impeller, suitable for low to medium pressure applications, efficiently delivering high flow rates. In contrast, multi-stage pumps feature multiple impellers in series, enabling them to achieve higher pressures, making them ideal for applications requiring significant elevation or resistance, such as water supply and industrial processes. Both types play crucial roles across various industries, catering to specific operational needs.</p></p>
