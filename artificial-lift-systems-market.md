<p><p>The artificial lift systems market includes various technologies designed to enhance production in oil and gas extraction. Rod lift systems use a pump jack to mechanically lift fluids, while Electric Submersible Pumps (ESP) utilize electric motors for deep extraction. Progressive Cavity Pumps (PCP) move fluids through a helical rotor mechanism, and hydraulic pumps rely on surface pressure to lift fluids. Gas lift systems inject gas to reduce fluid density, facilitating flow, whereas plunger lifts utilize a plunger mechanism to enhance production efficiently.</p></p>
