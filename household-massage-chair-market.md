<p><p>The household massage chair market features two primary types: rail massage chairs and trackless massage chairs. Rail massage chairs utilize fixed tracks to guide the massage mechanism, providing consistent and targeted pressure along the body’s contours. In contrast, trackless massage chairs offer more flexibility and a wider range of motion, allowing for a more customizable massage experience. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences, with rail models typically emphasizing specific techniques and trackless models promoting versatility and broader coverage.</p></p>
