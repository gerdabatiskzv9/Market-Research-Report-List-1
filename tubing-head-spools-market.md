<p><p>Tubing head spools are essential components in the oil and gas industry, primarily used to support well completion. In the single completion model, a single tubing string is utilized for oil or gas extraction, allowing straightforward management of production. Conversely, the dual completion model accommodates two tubing strings within one well, facilitating simultaneous production from multiple zones. This increases efficiency and maximizes resource recovery while reducing operational costs, making both models pivotal for optimizing well performance and enhancing production strategies.</p></p>
