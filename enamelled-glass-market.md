<p><p>The enamelled glass market is segmented into three primary types: transparent, translucent, and opaque. Transparent enamelled glass allows clear visibility while providing added durability, making it ideal for windows and facades. Translucent enamelled glass diffuses light, offering privacy without complete obstruction, suitable for partitions and decorative applications. Opaque enamelled glass blocks light entirely, making it perfect for screens or areas needing enhanced privacy and aesthetic appeal. Each type serves distinct architectural and design purposes in various industries.</p></p>
