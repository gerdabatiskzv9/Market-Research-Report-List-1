<p><p>The Automotive Pre-Crash Seatbelt Market includes two main types: **Preload Type Belt** and **Preroll Preload Type Belt**. Preload Type Belts tighten automatically before a collision, reducing occupant movement and enhancing safety. Preroll Preload Type Belts combine pre-tensioning with a retracting mechanism that rolls back the belt slightly before tightening, offering smoother and more precise restraint. Both systems aim to minimize injury by securing passengers effectively during sudden impacts, with Preroll Preload providing advanced control and comfort.</p></p>
