<p><p>The Aircraft Shock Absorbers Market includes Mechanical and Hydraulic types. Mechanical shock absorbers use springs or elastomeric materials to absorb and dissipate landing impact energy, offering simplicity and reliability. Hydraulic shock absorbers utilize fluid compression and damping mechanisms to provide smoother energy absorption, enhancing performance and comfort. Both types are critical for reducing stress on aircraft structures and ensuring safe landings. The choice depends on aircraft size, operational requirements, and desired performance characteristics.</p></p>
