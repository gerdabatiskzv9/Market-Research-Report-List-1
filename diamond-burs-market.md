<p><p>The diamond burs market comprises three primary types based on shank design: Long Straight Shank, Latch-type Shank, and Friction Grip Shank. Latch-type Shank burs feature a mechanism that securely grips the bur, offering stability during procedures. Friction Grip Shank burs are known for their ease of use and quick change capabilities, making them popular in dental practices for efficient workflow. Each type serves specific needs in dental and industrial applications.</p></p>
