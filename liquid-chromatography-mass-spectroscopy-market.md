<p><p>The Liquid Chromatography-Mass Spectroscopy (LC-MS) market comprises several key types, each catering to specific analytical needs. Triple Quadrupole systems are popular for their sensitivity and quantitative analysis capabilities, ideal for detecting trace levels of compounds. Time of Flight (ToF) instruments provide high-resolution mass measurements, excelling in complex mixtures. Quadrupole-Time of Flight integrates both technologies, offering precise quantification and structural information. Other systems may include ion trap and orbitrap technologies, providing specialized applications in research and industry.</p></p>
