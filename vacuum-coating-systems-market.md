<p><p>The vacuum coating systems market encompasses various technologies, including evaporation, sputtering, ion plating, and chemical vapor deposition (CVD). </p><p>Evaporation involves transforming materials into vapor, allowing them to condense on substrates. Sputtering uses energetic ions to dislodge atoms from a target material, depositing them onto a surface. Ion plating combines sputtering with vapor deposition for improved adhesion. CVD involves chemical reactions that deposit thin films on substrates. Other methods may include spray coating and atomic layer deposition, each catering to specific industry needs and applications.</p></p>
