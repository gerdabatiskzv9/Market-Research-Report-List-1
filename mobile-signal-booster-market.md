<p><p>The mobile signal booster market is primarily divided into two types: analog signal boosters and digital signal boosters. Analog signal boosters enhance existing cellular signals using simple amplification techniques, making them suitable for small areas with weak reception. In contrast, digital signal boosters employ advanced technology to process and amplify signals, providing superior performance and coverage. They are ideal for larger spaces and can support multiple users simultaneously, ensuring more reliable connectivity in various environments.</p></p>
