<p><strong>The Low-Light Imaging Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Charge Coupled Device</li><li>Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor Transistor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low-light imaging technology market comprises primarily two types: Charge Coupled Device (CCD) and Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor (CMOS) sensors. CCD sensors excel in low-light conditions due to their high sensitivity and low noise, making them ideal for applications like astronomy and professional photography. Conversely, CMOS sensors are increasingly favored for their lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and integration capabilities, suitable for consumer electronics, surveillance, and automotive applications. Each technology offers distinct advantages, catering to various industry needs.</p></p>
