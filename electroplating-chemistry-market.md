<p><ul><li>Pretreatment Agent</li><li>Electroplating Additive</li><li>Post-treatment Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electroplating chemistry market consists of three main types: pretreatment agents, electroplating additives, and post-treatment agents. </p><p>Pretreatment agents are chemicals used to clean and prepare surfaces prior to electroplating, ensuring better adhesion and quality. Electroplating additives enhance the electroplating process by improving deposit characteristics such as appearance and uniformity. Post-treatment agents are applied after electroplating to enhance durability, corrosion resistance, and aesthetic properties of the finished surface. Together, these components optimize the electroplating process and improve product performance.</p></p>
