<p><p>The truck camshaft market includes three main types: Cast Camshafts, Assembled Camshafts, and Forged Camshafts. Cast Camshafts are cost-effective and widely used, made by pouring molten metal into molds, suitable for standard applications. Assembled Camshafts are built by combining separate components, offering flexibility and customization for specific engine needs. Forged Camshafts are high-performance options, created by shaping metal under extreme pressure, ensuring superior strength and durability for heavy-duty or high-stress environments. Each type caters to different trucking requirements, balancing cost, performance, and application demands.</p></p>
