<p><p>The personal cooling system market encompasses various technologies designed for individual temperature regulation. Evaporative cooling systems utilize water evaporation to cool the air, while ambient air systems rely on regular airflow to lower body temperature. Gel/ice pack vests use frozen elements for immediate cooling, and phase change material (PCM) garments incorporate materials that absorb heat at specific temperatures, providing prolonged cooling. Liquid circulating systems involve circulating cold fluids through clothing or pads, ensuring consistent, targeted temperature control for the user.</p></p>
