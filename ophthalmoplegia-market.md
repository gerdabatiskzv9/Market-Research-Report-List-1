<p><p>Ophthalmoplegia refers to paralysis or weakness of the eye muscles, affecting eye movement. The external ophthalmoplegia market focuses on conditions that impair the extraocular muscles, leading to difficulty in eye coordination and positioning. In contrast, the internuclear ophthalmoplegia market addresses disruptions in the pathways between the brainstem and eye muscles, often resulting in issues like double vision and misalignment. Both markets target treatment options, diagnostics, and rehabilitation strategies to enhance patient outcomes and quality of life.</p></p>
