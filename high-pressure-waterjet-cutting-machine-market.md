<p><p>The high-pressure waterjet cutting machine market features various types, including cantilever and gantry systems. Cantilever machines have a single support arm, allowing for flexibility and easier access to workpieces, making them ideal for smaller operations. In contrast, gantry systems use a larger, more stable frame that spans the cutting area, delivering high precision and efficiency for larger or complex projects. Both types cater to different industrial needs, enhancing cutting capabilities across diverse applications.</p></p>
