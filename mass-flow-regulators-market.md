<p><p>Mass flow regulators (MFRs) are crucial in maintaining precise gas flow rates in various applications. The market for MFRs is divided into two main types: direct and indirect. </p><p>Direct type MFRs provide immediate response to pressure changes, offering high accuracy and stability for precise flow control. In contrast, indirect type MFRs utilize a feedback mechanism that adjusts flow rates based on differential pressure, making them suitable for less critical applications. Both types serve distinct needs across industries such as healthcare, pharmaceuticals, and manufacturing.</p></p>
