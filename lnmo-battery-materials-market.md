<p><ul><li>Electrode Sheets</li><li>Electrode Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LNMO (Lithium Nickel Manganese Oxide) battery materials market is categorized into two primary types: electrode sheets and electrode powders. Electrode sheets are pre-manufactured layers that provide a ready-to-use form for battery assembly, offering advantages in scalability and uniformity. In contrast, electrode powders are fine particles used to create customized electrode formulations, allowing for superior design flexibility and performance optimization. These materials are essential for enhancing battery efficiency, energy density, and overall longevity in energy storage applications.</p></p>
