<p><ul><li>Float-Feed Carburetor</li><li>Diaphragm Carburetor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Primary Carburetor market features two main types: Float-Feed Carburetors and Diaphragm Carburetors. Float-Feed Carburetors use a float mechanism to maintain a constant fuel level, ensuring consistent fuel delivery for optimal engine performance. In contrast, Diaphragm Carburetors utilize a flexible diaphragm that responds to engine vacuum changes, regulating fuel flow based on demand. These carburetor types cater to different engine requirements, impacting fuel efficiency, power output, and overall performance across various applications in automotive and small engine markets.</p></p>
