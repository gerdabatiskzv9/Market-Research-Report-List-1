<p><strong>The LDI Laser Direct Imaging Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polygon Mirror 365nm</li><li>DMD 405nm</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LDI (Laser Direct Imaging) machines market comprises various technologies, primarily characterized by their imaging wavelengths. The Polygon Mirror 365nm systems utilize a rotating polygon mirror to efficiently project high-resolution images onto substrates, making them suitable for demanding applications in PCB manufacturing. In contrast, DMD (Digital Micromirror Device) 405nm systems use micro mirrors to achieve precise image projection, offering flexibility and enhanced image quality. Both types cater to the growing demand for accurate, high-speed imaging solutions in electronics and other industries.</p></p>
