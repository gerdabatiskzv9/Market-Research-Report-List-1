<p><strong>The Flow Cytometry Instruments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cell Analyzers</li><li>Cell Sorters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flow cytometry instruments market is categorized into two main types: cell analyzers and cell sorters. Cell analyzers are used to measure various physical and chemical characteristics of cells as they flow in a fluid stream, providing quantitative data for cell populations. Cell sorters, on the other hand, can separate and isolate specific cell types based on their characteristics, allowing for further experimentation. Both types play critical roles in research, clinical diagnostics, and therapeutic development.</p></p>
