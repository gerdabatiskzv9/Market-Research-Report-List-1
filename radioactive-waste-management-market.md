<p><p>Radioactive waste management encompasses various market types based on the waste's radioactivity level. Exempt waste and Very Low-Level Waste (VLLW) are minimally radioactive and may require limited regulatory oversight. Low-Level Waste (LLW) contains higher radioactivity and is managed through specialized disposal facilities. Intermediate-Level Waste (ILW) has a greater radiological hazard and necessitates robust containment. High-Level Waste (HLW), highly radioactive and heat-generating, demands significant safety measures and long-term storage solutions. Each category requires tailored management strategies to ensure environmental safety.</p></p>
