<p><p>The Low-E Vacuum Glass market comprises three main types based on their construction. Single Low-E glass features a single low-emissivity coating, offering basic thermal efficiency. Double Low-E glass incorporates two layers of low-emissivity coatings between the panes, enhancing insulation and energy efficiency. Triple Low-E glass includes three layers of coatings, providing superior thermal performance and noise reduction. Each type caters to varying insulation needs, with higher layers translating to better energy savings and comfort in residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
