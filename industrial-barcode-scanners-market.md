<p><p>Industrial barcode scanners are essential tools in various sectors for inventory management and quality control. The market includes three primary types: </p><p>1. **Laser Scanners**: These devices use lasers to read 1D barcodes quickly and accurately, ideal for high-speed environments. </p><p>2. **2D Imagers**: Capable of scanning both 1D and 2D barcodes, these versatile scanners utilize camera technology to capture images, allowing for greater data throughput. </p><p>3. **Linear Imagers**: These are simpler and typically less expensive than laser scanners, effectively reading 1D barcodes using a linear sensor. Each type serves distinct operational needs in industrial settings.</p></p>
