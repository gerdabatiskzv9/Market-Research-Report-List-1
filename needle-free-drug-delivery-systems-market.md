<p><strong>The Needle-Free Drug Delivery Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Jet-Based</li><li>Spring-Based</li><li>Laser-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Needle-free drug delivery systems are categorized into three main types: jet-based, spring-based, and laser-based. </p><p>Jet-based systems use high-pressure jets to propel medication through the skin, enabling rapid absorption. Spring-based systems utilize mechanical springs to deliver doses accurately and swiftly. Laser-based devices employ focused energy to create micro-channels in the skin, facilitating drug penetration. These innovative methods aim to enhance patient comfort, improve adherence, and reduce the risks associated with traditional needle-based injection methods.</p></p>
