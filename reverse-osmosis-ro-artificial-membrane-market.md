<p><p>The Reverse Osmosis (RO) artificial membrane market is primarily categorized into two types: cellulose-based membranes and thin film composite membranes. Cellulose-based membranes are derived from natural materials, offering good permeability and lower fouling tendencies, making them suitable for applications like residential water purification. In contrast, thin film composite membranes are engineered for higher efficiency and durability, featuring superior rejection rates for contaminants, making them ideal for industrial and municipal water treatment processes. Both types play crucial roles in providing clean water solutions.</p></p>
