<p><p>The MIDI cables market is divided into two primary types: single cables and multicore cables. Single cables are designed for straightforward connections between individual MIDI devices, providing simplicity and ease of use for musicians and producers. Multicore cables, on the other hand, bundle multiple MIDI connections into a single cable, ideal for complex setups in studios and live performances. This type reduces cable clutter and ensures efficient signal management, catering to professionals who require versatility and organization in their gear arrangements.</p></p>
