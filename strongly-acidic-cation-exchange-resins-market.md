<p><p>The strongly acidic cation exchange resins market is primarily divided into two types: gel strongly acid cation exchange resins and macroporous strongly acid cation exchange resins. Gel resins feature a dense, uniform structure that offers high ion exchange capacity and selectivity, making them suitable for various applications, including water treatment and chemical processing. Macroporous resins possess a more open structure with larger pores, enhancing mass transfer and accessibility, which is advantageous for applications requiring rapid ion exchange and higher flow rates.</p></p>
