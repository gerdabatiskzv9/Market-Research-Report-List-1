<p><p>The passenger scanners market comprises several types: X-ray scanners, millimeter wave scanners, and other technologies. X-ray scanners utilize electromagnetic radiation to produce images of the contents hidden within baggage, ensuring security at airports and transportation hubs. Millimeter wave scanners, on the other hand, use non-ionizing radiation to detect concealed objects on a person's body, enhancing passenger screening without risking exposure to harmful radiation. Other scanning technologies may include advanced imaging systems and biological detection devices, all aimed at enhancing airport security protocols.</p></p>
