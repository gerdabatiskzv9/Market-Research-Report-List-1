<p><strong>The Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Internal Gear Pump</li><li>External Gear Pump</li><li>Rotary Vane Pump</li><li>Screw Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pump market encompasses various types, each serving distinct applications. Internal gear pumps utilize interlocking gears to move fluids, ideal for handling viscous substances. External gear pumps feature two gears rotating against each other, suitable for continuous flow in lower viscosity applications. Rotary vane pumps use movable vanes within a rotor to create suction, effective for volatile liquids. Screw pumps employ rotating screws to move fluids, offering high efficiency and reliability for both low and high viscosity materials.</p></p>
