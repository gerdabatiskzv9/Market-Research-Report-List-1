<p><p>The metal stamping equipment market encompasses various processes, including blanking, embossing, bending, coining, and flanging. Blanking involves cutting out shapes from metal sheets, while embossing creates raised designs or patterns. Bending shapes metal by applying force to achieve desired angles. Coining compresses metal to create precise shapes or details. Flanging involves creating raised edges or rims on metal parts. Each technique serves distinct applications across industries, contributing to the versatility and efficiency of metal fabrication processes.</p></p>
